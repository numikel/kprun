## [0.3.1] - TBD

### Security

- Hardened the env-injection blocklist: case-insensitive prefix matching now
  blocks the parameterized `LD_*`, `DYLD_*`, `GIT_*`, and `BASH_FUNC_*`
  families, and the exact-match list gains `NODE_EXTRA_CA_CERTS`,
  `PYTHONHOME`, `PYTHONINSPECT`, `PERL5LIB`, `PERL5OPT`, `RUBYLIB`, `RUBYOPT`,
  `JAVA_TOOL_OPTIONS`, `JDK_JAVA_OPTIONS`, `_JAVA_OPTIONS`, `LESSOPEN`, and
  `LESSCLOSE`. `GITHUB_*` variables remain injectable. SECURITY.md documents
  the strategy and recommends `--clean-env` for untrusted children.
